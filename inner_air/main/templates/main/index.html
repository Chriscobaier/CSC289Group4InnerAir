Your Personal Breathing Exercise Companion

Wondering why there's an entire site for breathing?

That is a great question. Here you will find a multitude of exercises, techniques and information regarding breathing. Let us tell you why you should take a special interest in how something you do everyday can do more than just keep you alive.

Exercise

Breathing exercises help slow down your breathing patterns and improve lung efficiency particularly if you have asthma, COPD, or other conditions that can cause shortness of breath.

Meditate

Correctly learning to breathe can be an important part of your whole body, not just your lungs. Here you will find breathing techniques designed to help reduce stress and provide other positive benefits such as lowering blood pressure and improving sleep quality.

Relaxation

Mindful breathing techniques can have a positive effect on your mind as well as the physical benefits mentioned previously. Proper breathing can lower stress and anxiety.
