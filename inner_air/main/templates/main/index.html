Your Personal Breathing Exercise Companion

Wondering why there's an entire site for breathing?

That is a great question. Here you will find a multitude of exercises, techniques and information regarding breathing. Let us tell you why you should take a special interest in how something you do everyday can do more than just keep you alive.

Exercise

Proper breathing techniques during exercise can help you breathe more efficiently, allowing you to take in more oxygen and remove more carbon dioxide. This can help you maintain physical activity for longer periods of time, improve your cardiovascular health and increase your endurance. Along with that, they help to maximize the efficiency of your movements, allowing you to perform exercises with greater ease and accuracy. This can improve your overall physical performance, leading to better results in your workouts.

Meditate

Correctly learning to breathe can be an important part of your whole body, not just your lungs. Here you will find breathing techniques designed to help reduce stress and provide other positive benefits such as lowering blood pressure and improving sleep quality. Focusing on the breath can help you become more aware of your thoughts, feelings, and physical sensations. This can help you better understand yourself and develop greater self-awareness.

Relaxation

Mindful breathing techniques can have a positive effect on your mind as well as the physical benefits mentioned previously. Breathing exercises can help reduce the amount of cortisol (a stress hormone) in your body, leading to a decrease in feelings of stress and anxiety. This can help you feel more relaxed and centered during your workouts, making it easier to stay motivated and focused.
